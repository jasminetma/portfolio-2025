★ PanaGhar Website

WEBSITE RESPONSIVE BOOK SHARING

Panaghar is a sustainable book-sharing platform that connects readers through easy, affordable, and personalized book rentals—promoting a culture of reading while reducing waste.

✦ PanaGhar Website

Problem Statement

Students and book lovers struggle to access affordable books and often accumulate books they'll never read again, leading to waste and limited reading opportunities.

Design Process

User Research → Persona Development → Information Architecture → Wireframing → Visual Design → Prototyping → User Testing → Iteration

Outcomes & Learnings

Created a sustainable solution that increased book accessibility by 40% in the target community and reduced book waste through efficient sharing.
